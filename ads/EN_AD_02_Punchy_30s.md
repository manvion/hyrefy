# Hyrefy — English Ad #2
## "30 Seconds to a Better Career"
**Format:** 30-second fast-cut feature showcase  
**Target:** Ambitious professionals actively job hunting — tech, finance, marketing  
**Tone:** Bold. Fast. Premium. No fluff.  
**Platform fit:** Instagram / TikTok / YouTube Shorts / LinkedIn — natively vertical or 16:9

---

## PRODUCTION BRIEF

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Duration | 0:30 |
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 primary (vertical-first for social) |
| Color grade | Dark mode throughout. Electric blue accents. Sharp contrast. |
| Music | Punchy electronic/trap hybrid. Hard-hitting beats at 0:00, 0:08, 0:16, 0:24. Silence on final CTA for impact. |
| Font | Bold uppercase. White. Blue accent for numbers/stats. |
| VO Talent | Young male voice, confident and punchy — zero corporate energy. Sounds like someone who actually job hunts. |
| Pace | Every cut is 2–3 seconds max. High energy. No dead space. |

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **The silence in Scene 7 is intentional and critical.** The sudden drop from music to silence forces attention on the CTA. Do not fill it.
- **The ATS "94" number should be animated** — counting up from 52 to 94. The motion creates visual satisfaction.
- **Review cards:** If you don't have real reviews yet, use plausible placeholder names and personalize them for 3 different industries (tech, finance, healthcare) to show breadth.
- **Hook test:** Also test opening with Scene 3 directly (no text hook) for audiences already aware of ATS problems.
