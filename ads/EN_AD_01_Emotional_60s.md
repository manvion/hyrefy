# Hyrefy — English Ad #1
## "The Resume That Gets You In"
**Format:** 60-second emotional storytelling  
**Target:** Job seekers who have been applying with no results  
**Tone:** Empathetic → Empowering → Aspirational  
**Platform fit:** YouTube pre-roll, LinkedIn Video, Instagram Reels (vertical crop available)

---

## PRODUCTION BRIEF

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Duration | 0:60 |
| Aspect ratio | 16:9 primary / 9:16 crop for mobile |
| Color grade | Deep navy → warm gold transition as the story resolves |
| Music | Starts slow piano + ambient tension (0–20s) → builds with strings + light percussion (20–45s) → full uplifting orchestral swell (45–60s) |
| Font | Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar). White on dark. Gold accent for brand moments |
| VO Talent | Warm, confident 30s female voice — not corporate, not salesy. Sounds like a mentor |

---

## SCRIPT — SCENE BY SCENE

---

### SCENE 1 — 0:00–0:05 | THE STRUGGLE
**Visual:**  
Tight close-up of hands typing on a laptop. Faint blue screen glow. Night. Energy drinks on the desk. A browser tab with "Job Application — Submitted" refreshes. Notification: "We have moved forward with other candidates."

**Color:** Desaturated blue-grey. Cold. Lonely.

**Music:** Quiet, slow piano note. Almost melancholic.

**On-screen text:** *(no text — pure visual)*

**VO:**
> "You've sent 47 applications this month."

---

### SCENE 2 — 0:05–0:12 | REJECTION FEELS REAL
**Visual:**  
Close-up of phone screen. Email preview: "Thank you for applying, but..." Scroll down — another one. Scroll — another. The person's face, slightly out of focus in the background, looking tired.

**Color:** Still cold. Slightly blurred edges.

**Music:** Same piano — a second note added.

**On-screen text:** *(none)*

**VO:**
> "You've rewritten your resume six times."  
> "You know you're qualified."  
> "So why isn't it working?"

---

### SCENE 3 — 0:12–0:18 | THE PROBLEM REVEALED
**Visual:**  
Split screen: Left — a polished resume. Right — a job description. Two red circles appear — the keywords in the job description that are MISSING from the resume. Simple, clean graphic overlay.

**Color:** Slightly warmer now.

**On-screen text:**  
`"75% of resumes never reach a human reader"`  
`"They're rejected by ATS software before anyone sees them."`  
*(small, clean white text, bottom third)*

**VO:**
> "Most resumes are filtered out before a single human reads them."  
> "Not because you're not qualified."  
> "Because your resume doesn't speak the right language."

---

### SCENE 4 — 0:18–0:26 | THE TURN — INTRODUCING HYREFY
**Visual:**  
Smooth transition — the cold blue world dissolves into a warm, modern interface. Hyrefy dashboard appears. Not a screen recording — a cinematic mock-up. The user types a job description. The AI starts working. A tailored resume appears in real time, words streaming in like it's being written.

**Color:** Warm gradients. Deep navy + gold accent. Premium feel.

**Music:** Strings enter. Gentle build begins.

**On-screen text:**  
`hyrefy.com`  
`Your AI career platform.`

**VO:**
> "Hyrefy reads the job description."  
> "Finds exactly what they're looking for."  
> "And rewrites your resume to match — in seconds."

---

### SCENE 5 — 0:26–0:38 | FEATURE SHOWCASE — FAST, VISUAL
**Visual:**  
Three rapid cinematic cuts, each 3–4 seconds:

**Cut A:** Resume being tailored for a specific role. "Senior Product Manager at Google" — resume transforms. Keywords highlighted in green as they're woven in.  
`ON SCREEN: "AI Resume Tailoring"`

**Cut B:** Cover letter appearing on screen, personalized paragraphs forming. Company logo (blurred/generic) in corner.  
`ON SCREEN: "Personalized Cover Letter"`

**Cut C:** ATS score ring animating from 54 → 91. Green. "Strong Match."  
`ON SCREEN: "ATS Score: 91/100"`

**Music:** Building. Momentum growing.

**VO:**
> "A tailored resume for every job."  
> "A cover letter that actually sounds like you."  
> "And an ATS score that tells you exactly where you stand."

---

### SCENE 6 — 0:38–0:48 | THE OUTCOME — PERSON'S JOURNEY
**Visual:**  
Same person from Scene 1 — but now it's morning. Bright light. They're dressed professionally, sitting across from an interviewer. The interviewer nods, smiles, leans forward. The person is confident, engaged.

*(No product shown — pure human moment)*

**Color:** Full warmth. Golden hour light.

**Music:** Orchestral swell begins.

**On-screen text:** *(none — let the visuals breathe)*

**VO:**
> "Because the goal was never just a better resume."  
> "It was getting into that room."

---

### SCENE 7 — 0:48–0:55 | GLOBAL REACH
**Visual:**  
A rotating globe with soft light points appearing: Toronto, London, Paris, Sydney, Mumbai, Dubai. Each point pulses. The camera pulls back — then cuts to the Hyrefy interface showing country flags: 🇺🇸 🇨🇦 🇬🇧 🇦🇺 🇫🇷 🇮🇳

**On-screen text:**  
`"Tailored for 9 countries. Formatted for your market."`

**VO:**
> "Whether you're applying in Canada, the UK, France, or India —  
> Hyrefy knows exactly how resumes work in your market."

---

### SCENE 8 — 0:55–1:00 | CTA — CLOSE
**Visual:**  
Clean, dark background. The Hyrefy logo appears center screen with a soft gold glow. Below it, a single clean CTA button animates in.

**Music:** Final orchestral hit. Fades to silence.

**On-screen text:**  
`hyrefy.com`  
`Free to start. Takes 2 minutes.`

**VO:**
> "Stop rewriting. Start getting hired."  
> "Hyrefy — your AI career platform."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Key graphic:** The ATS score ring animating from low → high is the most shareable moment. Consider cutting this into a 6-second standalone social clip.
- **Thumbnail:** Scene 6 (interview room with the confident candidate) + "47 Applications. 0 Interviews. This changed everything." headline.
- **Mobile crop (9:16):** Remove Scene 7 globe visual. Keep all VO. Use close-up text overlays throughout.
- **A/B test hook:** Test Scene 1 vs. opening directly on Scene 4 (product reveal) for different audiences.
